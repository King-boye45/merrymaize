About Us

Mission and Vision
MERRY INDUSTRY ltd.’s mission is “ To foster the economic and social development of Rwanda through actively pursuing economically viable, socially desirable and commercially profitable opportunities in production of finished Maize flour Processing of the industries in Rwanda.”
MERRY INDUSTRY Ltd.’s vision is “To become the leading vendor of finished Maize flour Processing of the industry Company in Rwanda with the highest quality with unique Maize flour that can be sold commercially to distributors and retailers as well as to private customers.”

Goals
MERRY INDUSTRY ltd is focused on achieving the following goals:
•Making and Maize flour in different categories for local, regional and international markets,
•Availing diverse Maize flour to customers in their respective segments,
•Create job opportunities for staff and technicians, promote financial mobility and
•Increasing availability of finished Maize flour Processing of the industry.
•Providing affordable Maize flour Processing.
•Becoming the premier providers of finished Maize flour Processing of the industry in Rwanda in the medium to long term;
•Providing world class, superior customer service to its clientele; and expanding the company’s expertise in Manufacturing of finished Maize flour Processing of the industry.
